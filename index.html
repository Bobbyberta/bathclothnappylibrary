Welcome to Bath Cloth Nappy Library

What We Offer

We hire out two types of cloth nappy kits to suit your needs

Newborn Kit

Perfect for parents of little ones aged 0-6 months. This kit includes a range of reusable nappies designed to fit your baby snugly during their first months.

Learn more about the Newborn Kit

Birth-to-Potty Kit

Designed for babies 6+ months, this kit features adjustable nappies that grow with your child and can be used until potty training.

Discover the Birth-to-Potty Kit

Each kit comes with clear instructions and access to our support team for any questions or advice
