Frequently Asked Questions

How long can I keep a kit?

Newborn kits can be hired for as long as your baby fits the nappies, if you are using them!

For the Birth-to-Potty kit, we recommend hiring for 2 weeks. This gives you enough time to try different nappies and see what works best for your baby.

What if my baby doesn't like them?

No problem! That's exactly why we offer hire kits. You can try them risk-free and return them if they're not working for your family.

Do I need to wash them before returning?

Yes, please return the nappies clean and dry. We'll provide washing instructions and can give advice if you need help.
