About Us

The Bath Cloth Nappy Library is a volunteer-led, not-for-profit organisation. Our mission is to make reusable nappies easy, affordable, and accessible for families in Bath.

Our Mission

We run on donations and small hire fees, and all income goes back into keeping the service running. We're committed to helping Bath families reduce waste and save money while providing gentle, natural care for their babies.
